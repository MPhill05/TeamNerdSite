My journey into development began in high school and into college, starting with Pascal and later learning my way around Java development. I love being creative and after teaching myself how to produce music, I concluded learning web development shouldn't be too difficult. Curiosity lead me down a totally different path than my original goal of just making a website for my own music.


Technology

My goal was to be able to build sites that would leave an impression in a similar way that a good song does. I enjoy challenging myself and building apps that encourage exploration while conveying a clear message.


Web

I design sites using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks such as bootstrap and Angular 2. Recently I have been exploring building hybrid apps using Ionic for faster turn around and the ability to deploy on multiple platforms at once.


App Dev

Curiosity and creativity didn't stop at web development for me. I wanted to use my back end knowledge obtained in college with Java and decided to learn how to build Android Applications. I've developed various applications, but as I was learning Android development, I wanted to make apps that I could use. So I decided to learn how to make simple apps with xcode as well for iOS. I've built various apps from simple calculators, a tamagachi, and even a functioning social network.
